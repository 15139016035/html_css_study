落魄谷中寒风吹，春秋蝉鸣少年归。 荡魂山处石人泪，定仙游走魔向北。 逆流河上万仙退，爱情不敌坚持泪。 宿命天成命中败，仙尊悔而我不悔。

In Luo Po Valley, where the cold winds sweep, The Spring Autumn Cicada calls the youth from sleep. On Dang Hun Mountain, where stone statues weep, With Fixed Immortal Travel, the demon's path runs deep. On Reverse Flow River, ten thousand immortals bend, For love can't match the tears of a goal without end. Though heavenly Fate is itself destined to fall, The Venerables regretted, but I regret nothing at all.
